Overview

Everything needed to calculate trade size, measure profit and loss, estimate fees and keep a simple offline trade journal.

Position Calculator

Calculate the correct position size using either percentage risk or a fixed dollar amount. The app automatically detects whether the setup is Long or Short and shows position size, stop-loss distance, risk amount and reward metrics.

Profit & Loss

Measure gross and net profit after a trade has been closed. Optional trading fees are deducted automatically to display the real result.

Trading Fees

Estimate entry fees, exit fees and the total cost of opening and closing a trade before placing an order.

Saved Trades

Save planned trades and completed trades locally on your device. No account required and no information is uploaded anywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Every calculator works completely offline. The only optional internet request is for the announcement banner.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Position Sizer works without creating an account or signing in.

Where are my saved trades stored?

All saved trades remain on your device. They are never uploaded to a server.

Which markets does it support?

The application can be used for crypto, forex and other markets where position size, risk and reward calculations are needed.
